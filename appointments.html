Pre-Treatment Instructions

Please follow these instructions before your appointment to ensure the best experience.

General Instructions

  • Please arrive 10-15 minutes before your scheduled appointment time
  • Brush your teeth before coming to the clinic
  • Avoid eating a heavy meal 2 hours before your procedure
  • Inform us of any medications you're currently taking
  • Bring your ID and insurance information if applicable

For Specific Procedures

  • Take prescribed antibiotics if provided
  • Eat a light meal before your appointment
  • Avoid alcohol 24 hours before the procedure
  • Inform your dentist about any health conditions
  • Inform us if you have any medical conditions
  • Arrange for someone to drive you home after the procedure
  • Wear comfortable clothing
  • Avoid smoking for at least 12 hours before extraction
  • Brush and floss before your appointment
  • Provide your complete dental history
  • Inform the hygienist of any sensitive areas
