FAQs About General Dentistry

How often should I visit the dentist?

We recommend at least twice a year for preventive check-ups and cleanings.

Are dental check-ups painful?

Not at all! Our team ensures a comfortable and gentle experience during your visit.

Do I need dental X-rays every year?

Not always. X-rays are recommended based on your age, risk factors, and dental history.
